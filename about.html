State Of The Art Gym

At Muscle Strength Lab, we're more than just a fitness center - we're the community built on passion, perseverance, and personal growth. Whether you're lifting for the first time or training for your next competition, our space is designed to support every level of fitness.

Hyrox

HYROX classes are designed to prepare participants for the HYROX fitness race, combining running and functional fitness exercises. These classes incorporate a mix of strength training, high-intensity interval training (HIIT), and endurance exercises. They typically involve a combination of running (8km total, broken into 1km intervals) and functional workouts like sled pushes, rowing, and wall balls.

Spin Classes

Spin classes, also known as spinning or cycle classes, are a high-intensity workout conducted on stationary bikes in a group setting. These classes offer a low-impact, cardiovascular workout that can be enjoyed by people of various fitness levels, from beginners to experienced cyclists.
